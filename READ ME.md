**Game title**: Boba Blastin

Boba Blastin is a 3rd person platformer with shooting mechanics. The player plays as a boba ball with a gun. Your goal is to escape the cup you are in by collecting all the straw pieces. Your enemies are other boba balls and boba ingredients.
